OUR STORY

VIETNAMESE ROOTS

Born in the vibrant city of Da Nang, Vietnam. We bring authentic Vietnamese coffee culture with traditional brewing methods and local coffee beans to create an genuine experience.

AUTHENTIC TRADITION

No pretense, pure tradition. Vietnamese coffee beans, traditional phin brewing, condensed milk, and time-honored recipes. Quality coffee the Vietnamese way.

LOCAL SANCTUARY

More than coffee – we're your neighborhood gathering place. A space where tradition meets comfort, where friends meet over perfectly brewed Vietnamese coffee.
